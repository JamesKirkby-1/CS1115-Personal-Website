Facts about Bandon Grammar School

Bandon Grammar School is a private secondary school located in Bandon, County Cork, Ireland. It is one of the oldest schools in ireland, founded circa 1642.

The school has one of the most comprehensive programmes of activities and sports available in Ireland, offering sports such as:

  • Rugby
  • Hockey
  • Basketball
  • Cricket
  • Track & Field

Many of its past and present pupils have reached provincial and international level in sports.
